## Welcome to the McMaster Math & Statistics Graduate Society!
The purpose of this association is to host social events to foster a sense of community within the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at McMaster. This association also acts as a way to facilitate communication between graduate students and the Chair of the Department of Mathematics and Statistics.
